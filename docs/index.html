Lehigh Valley Air Quality

Project summary:

PM2.5 describes fine inhalable particles, with diameters that are generally 2.5 micrometers and smaller. Particles of this size can have both health and environmental effects. The following project focuses on PM2.5 data collected throughout the Lehigh Valley.

Two sources of PM2.5 data throughout the Lehigh Valley:

  1. Data collected by the Pennsylvania Department of Environmental Protection at their Freemansburg monitoring site. Data can be accessed here.

  2. Data collected by a series of PurpleAir sensors located throughout the Lehigh Valley. Data can be accessed here.
